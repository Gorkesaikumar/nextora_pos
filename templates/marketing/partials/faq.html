FAQ

Questions? We have answers.

What is the free trial and how long does it last?

{% include "icons/chevron-down.html" with class="w-5 h-5 text-slate-400 transition-transform duration-200" %}

The free trial gives you full access to the Professional plan for 14 days with no credit card required. You can add your restaurant details, products, and staff and start taking real orders during this period. At the end of the trial, choose a paid plan or your account will be paused.

Does Nextora support GST and Indian billing standards?

{% include "icons/chevron-down.html" with class="w-5 h-5 text-slate-400 transition-transform duration-200" %}

Absolutely. Nextora is built specifically for the Indian market. It supports HSN/SAC codes, CGST/SGST/IGST breakdowns, GSTIN validation, and generates GST-compliant invoices. Our invoice format meets all requirements for filing returns.

Can I manage multiple restaurant branches?

{% include "icons/chevron-down.html" with class="w-5 h-5 text-slate-400 transition-transform duration-200" %}

Yes. The Professional plan supports up to 3 branches, and the Enterprise plan supports Staff and HR management. Each branch has its own menu, staff, inventory tracking, and reporting — all visible from a single owner dashboard.

How does the Kitchen Display System (KDS) work?

{% include "icons/chevron-down.html" with class="w-5 h-5 text-slate-400 transition-transform duration-200" %}

When a cashier places an order on the POS terminal, it appears instantly on the kitchen display screen. Kitchen staff can mark items as in progress and complete. You can configure multiple KDS screens per station (grill, drinks, desserts), and each screen only shows relevant items.

Is my data safe and backed up?

{% include "icons/chevron-down.html" with class="w-5 h-5 text-slate-400 transition-transform duration-200" %}

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest. We perform continuous backups with point-in-time recovery. Your data is hosted on ISO-27001 certified infrastructure and we maintain a 99.9% uptime SLA.

Can I import my existing menu and customer data?

{% include "icons/chevron-down.html" with class="w-5 h-5 text-slate-400 transition-transform duration-200" %}

Yes. We provide CSV/Excel import templates for products, inventory, customer lists, and suppliers. Our onboarding wizard guides you through the import process step by step, and our support team can assist if you have a large catalog.

What payment methods does the POS support?

{% include "icons/chevron-down.html" with class="w-5 h-5 text-slate-400 transition-transform duration-200" %}

Nextora POS supports cash, card (via external card readers), UPI, NEFT/RTGS, and various integrated payment gateways. You can configure multiple payment methods per branch and set the default for quick checkout.
